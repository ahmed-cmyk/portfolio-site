The problem my portfolio solves from a user’s perspective is helping potential freelance clients quickly determine whether I’m the right person to hire. Clients visiting my site are often looking for someone reliable, skilled, and easy to work with — they don’t want to waste time guessing my capabilities or professionalism. My portfolio addresses this by clearly showcasing my skills, experience, and past work in a way that builds trust and reduces friction. It makes it easy for them to understand who I am, what I do, and how to contact me, so they can confidently reach out for freelance project.
